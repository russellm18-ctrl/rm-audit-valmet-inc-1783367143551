// Findings

  • THE STRUCTURAL METADATA COLLAPSE: Your site deploys multi-language content across 13 language variants, yet your image assets carry no localized alt-text or hreflang-aligned JSON-LD schema to anchor them to regional search intent. A procurement engineer in Brazil searching "tissue machine rebuild solutions São Paulo" or a mill operations director in Finland querying "Bioneer dryer fabric supplier" is landing on a competitor because your Advantage NTTe and Bioneer product imagery exists as untagged decorative files with zero structured data connecting them to those high-intent queries. Your €5.2B revenue scale means the indexing gap isn't a minor oversight—it's systematically hemorrhaging inbound specification-stage leads across every region you operate in.
  • THE HIDDEN TRUST DEFICIT: The capital equipment buyer evaluating a full production line rebuild—like the JK Paper PM5 project you reference—needs to see operational proof before they engage your sales team, and your site gives them written case summaries without a single embedded process photograph, annotated machine section image, or before/after visual sequence. Industrial procurement committees at this level are defending a multi-million euro decision to a board; written outcome claims without photographic documentation of the physical installation read as unverifiable marketing copy. That trust gap converts a qualified mid-funnel evaluator into a phone call to your competitor.
  • THE COMPETITIVE EXPOSURE: Competitors serving the same pulp, tissue, and energy automation space—Andritz and Voith specifically—deploy structured visual libraries of commissioned process installations: timestamped, facility-identified, section-specific photography paired with technical specification overlays embedded directly into product pages. Your Sappi Gratkorn wash press upgrade and Opal digital services case studies are text-first with no accompanying installation photography, which means when a mill manager compares reference libraries side-by-side during vendor shortlisting, your documented proof of physical execution is absent. That single omission repositions you from proven supplier to aspirational vendor, and at contract values in the eight-figure range, even a 2% conversion rate shift represents tens of millions in lost orders annually.
  • THE PHOTO-FIRST UPGRADE: Commission a structured visual asset capture protocol tied directly to your active customer references—starting with installations like the Zhejiang Forest United Paper PM6 board machine and the Gratkorn fiberline—capturing process-stage photography, equipment close-ups, and operational environment shots with embedded EXIF location data, ISO-standard file naming, and product-line-specific alt-text mapped to your existing Solution Finder taxonomy. Each image asset gets deployed with JSON-LD Product and Service schema linking the visual to the corresponding segment (Biomaterial Solutions or Process Performance Solutions), the specific technology (Advantage NTTe, Bioneer, flow control), and the regional market served. This converts your reference library from a text archive into a structured, search-indexable visual proof system that does specification-stage selling before your sales team makes first contact.
